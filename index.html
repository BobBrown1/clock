Welcome to WhenIsLun.ch!

Wondering how much time is left in your schedule? Enter your schedule here and get customized results each time you load the page! This site is made to help you manage your schedule, whether you're in school or at work! Once you set your schedule, you'll be able to refresh the page without it being reset (as long as you don't clear your cache). If you spot any issues while using this site, you can open an issue in our github.

Schedule Time Format:

You should format your schedule times in the HH:MM AM/PM format. For example, 10:30 AM, 12:15 PM, and 3:45 PM.

We also allow the usage military time, but you will still have to include AM/PM . If you try to use AM in a military time after 13:00, your schedule will not be accepted.



Sharing Schedules:

Often someone else will have a similar (or the same) schedule as you or you will have different schedules every day. It can be annoying to have to enter the same schedule on multiple devices or enter a new one each day. Luckily, WhenIsLun.ch comes with a simple schedule sharing feature.

The sharing feature is very simple. To use it, follow the instructions below:

  1. Set your schedule on your primary device. If you don't want to reset your own schedule, simply enter the schedules into the input boxes without clicking submit.
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the main page and click Share Schedule. If you're schedule is not set, click the arrow next to the Submit button and select Get Share Link. This will open a modal where you can view and copy your schedule link.
  3. Click the Copy button and the link will be copied to your clipboard.

Once you've followed these steps, you can bookmark and/or share your schedule. Bookmarking your schedule will save you time if you have different schedules throughout your week. To bookmark these links, simply paste the URL into your browser and bookmark the page before clicking Return Home on the landing page. If you'd just like to share your schedule, simply paste the link into an email/text and send it to anyone you'd like!
